🎤 原创段子 · Original One-liners

  • America has 1 President. Microsoft has 200. And neither can get anything done.🔥
  • My title is Senior Software Engineer II. The "Senior" means I've been here long enough to know the code is bad but not long enough to leave.
  • At Microsoft, getting promoted doesn't mean more power. It means a longer email signature.🔥
  • Corporate titles are like fast food sizes. What used to be "Large" is now "Medium." What used to be "Manager" is now "Individual Contributor."
  • I met a VP at a party. I was impressed. Then he said he works at a bank. At banks, VP means you've been there 3 years and they ran out of raises.
  • Title inflation is why LinkedIn is fiction. "Head of Innovation" = one guy with a whiteboard. "Director of Strategy" = makes PowerPoints. "Chief Evangelist" = unemployed with confidence.🔥
  • My company gave everyone a title bump instead of a raise. Now I'm a Principal Engineer. I still can't afford rent, but my business card looks amazing.
  • In tech, "Staff Engineer" sounds important until you realize there are 400 of them. It's the participation trophy of job titles.
  • The US military has 4-star generals. Tech has 67-level engineers. Same energy, fewer push-ups.🔥
  • My mom tells relatives I'm a "Director" at Microsoft. I don't correct her. She doesn't need to know that 10,000 other people also have that title.CALLBACK
