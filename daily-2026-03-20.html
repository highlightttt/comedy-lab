香蕉 · No Washing Required
懒人逻辑：不想洗水果→选一个不用洗的水果。逻辑正确但荒谬。
I don't like wash fruits before and after eat them, that's why my favorite fruit is banana.

📚 参考段子 · Reference Bits

Halo Halo Family
Lazy teen的独特洗碗方式——懒人总能找到创造性的解决方案。

🎤 原创段子 · Original One-liners

  • I hate washing fruit. That's why my favorite fruit is banana. God already wrapped it for me.JESSE原版
  • Banana is nature's to-go meal. Built-in wrapper, no seeds, no knife needed. It's the only fruit designed by someone who actually eats fruit.
  • I pick all my food based on laziness. Banana over apple. Microwave over oven. Delivery over cooking. My diet is just a series of shortcuts.
  • People say "wash your fruit." I say "pick better fruit." You don't wash a banana. You don't wash an orange. Lazy isn't a problem — it's a filter.🔥
  • My mom said "always wash fruit before eating." So I stopped eating fruit. Problem solved.🔥
  • Grapes are a scam. 50 tiny fruits and you have to wash every single one. That's not a snack — that's a chore.
  • I eat bananas because they come in their own packaging. If apples came pre-peeled, I'd eat those too. I'm not lazy — I'm efficient.
  • My girlfriend buys strawberries. You have to wash them, cut the tops off, and eat them within 2 days or they rot. That's not food — that's a commitment.🔥
  • I rank fruit by effort. S-tier: banana. A-tier: orange. F-tier: pomegranate. If eating a fruit requires YouTube instructions, it's not food — it's a project.🔥
  • Coconut is the most aggressive fruit. You need a hammer to eat it. That's not a fruit — that's a threat.
